DISHA HISHAM
Cinematographer
Moustafa Hisham (Disha) is an Egyptian cinematographer and director. Beginning in photography at the age of 16, his practice expanded into illustration, graphic design, and 2D motion graphics — forming a layered visual perspective. This foundation led to a focus on cinema, developed through formal education and on-set experience. Work spans TV, documentaries, and commercials, with an emphasis on meaningful, visually expressive storytelling.

He has been involved in major productions within the cinematography department, including Tayea', Blue Elephant II, El Aref, and El Sett — working alongside leading cinematographers and directors, and shaping a strong visual foundation.

From Recce to Frame

With a strong visual foundation, recce pictures become the starting point of the preparation process — where observations evolve through hand-drawn lighting plans and 2D & 3D illustrations into practical cinematic frames.
